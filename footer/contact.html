Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

Can I contact a specific writer directly?

Writers can be reached through our editorial team. Please send your message to editorial@sportsdirect.com with the writer's name in the subject line.

How do I report a technical issue?

Please use the contact form above and select "Technical Issue" from the subject dropdown. Include details about your device, browser, and the problem you're experiencing.

Where can I submit a story tip?

We welcome story tips! Please email editorial@sportsdirect.com with your tip. Our editorial team reviews all submissions.

Do you accept guest contributions?

We occasionally accept guest articles from experts in the sports industry. Please email editorial@sportsdirect.com with your pitch and writing samples.
